Who is Jensyn?

Hello! I'm Jensyn Huynh, and I'm a Computer Science major in Texas A&M with a background in fashion design and animation. I hold a strong belief that art is indispensible, so I started my academic career in the art field. But as I delved deeper, I realized I wanted to develop art programs rather than doing actual art. This led me down the rabbithole of technology, and I became more and more fascinated by it.

Since making the switch to Computer Science, I have immersed myself in learning programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++. There were definitely challenges as I had absolutely no experience compared to my peers. But through these challenges, I learned that I am just as capable as any other computer scientist and grown a lot during my years as a student. I am driven by a desire to create tools that artists can use to express their creativity and add even more art into the world.
